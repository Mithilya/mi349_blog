Space Saving Hangers

By Mithilya Srinivasan
Published Sat October 15, 2022

Image Credit: Click Here

You might look at your closet and think, there is no more room to hang anymore of my clothes. A great way to hang more clothes and have a much more efficient use of a closet space are having space saving hangers. Space saving hangers allow you to hang several garments in slots, vertically or horizontally, on one single hanger rack. You can essentially place 5 or 6 plus clothing items on a single hanger rack. You can hang items such as your coats, shirts, pants, purses, accessories, and suits. This is very much ideal for places with small closets such as dorms, apartments, etc. Space saving hangers are great if you want to group certain items by season, weather, occasions, sizes and more. Closets can clutter up real fast with items and space saving hangers can create more room for not only clothing but other items as well. Beware of using wooden hangers, those can bulk up on the hangers. Be sure to use slim plastic hangers to really make the most use of your space. These space saving hangers are not only for clothes but can be used for other purposes as well. Let your imagination flow and customize this system to whatever suits your purpose. Magnetic Makeup Organizer (DIY)

By Mithilya Srinivasan
Published Sat October 15, 2022

Image Credit: Click Here

Counters can clutter up with several small makeup items leaving you with less space and a mess. In order to save space and organize your makeup, you can make your own magnetic makeup frame. The materials you will need for this project include 1 picture frame of any size, 1 thin magnet sheet that matches the size of your frame, and several small adhesive magnets corresponding to the size of your makeup. Simply place the magnet sheet inside the picture frame. Then, stick the adhesive side of the several small magnets on to your makeup items. Once all your makeup items have magnets on them, place all of your makeup items onto your magnetic makeup frame where they will stay off your counter and on the frame. This is also a great way to display your makeup and locate all of your items in a much more efficient and faster way. This project will only take about 20 minutes. The organizer is not only limited to makeup items, but can be versatile for other purposes as well. Let your imagination flow and customize this system to whatever suits your purpose.
